#  Verify presence of spelling or grammar errors.

Grammatical Error in Subject Line

The Error: The subject line reads "A Urgent: Your Bank Account Will Be Suspended!".
Why it is suspicious: This is a clear grammatical mistake. The correct article would be "An Urgent" or simply "Urgent." Major financial institutions use automated templates that are strictly proofread and would not contain basic article errors.


Awkward Phrasing and Terminology

"Please do not ignore this mail": The use of the word "mail" instead of "email" or "correspondence" is informal and less common in official corporate notices.
Logical Phrasing: The sentence "Please do not ignore this mail to avoid legal action" is clumsily constructed. A professional legal disclaimer would typically state, "Failure to respond may result in legal action," rather than the awkward construction used here.
